Top Exam Tips for Ethiopian Students

1. Understand the Curriculum

Study past papers, know what to expect, and focus on the areas with higher weight.

2. Make a Study Plan

Break subjects into daily chunks. Use a calendar or planner to stay on track.

3. Practice Regularly

Do daily problem solving, especially math, physics, and chemistry questions.

4. Use Video Tutorials

Watch ATC Tube lessons for clearer understanding and easy memorization.

5. Stay Healthy

Sleep well, drink water, and eat nutritious food to stay mentally sharp.
